Certifications are forcing functions, not credentials. Nobody has ever hired me because of a badge. But every certification forced me to sit with a domain long enough to stop guessing, and the guides on this page are what fell out of that process.

Everything here is public. The study material I wrote for myself turned out to be useful to other people, which was not the plan but is the better outcome.
