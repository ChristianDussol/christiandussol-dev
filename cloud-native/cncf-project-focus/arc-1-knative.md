# Episode 1: Knative

## Episode 1: Knative

**Vendor-Neutral Serverless on Kubernetes**

The first episode of the CNCF Project Focus series examines Knative as a serverless substrate: scale-to-zero economics, the split between Serving and Eventing and the governance challenge of running ephemeral workloads in a regulated environment.

### What the episode covers

* The Knative architecture and why it is not just "another FaaS"
* Scale-to-zero as a FinOps lever and the cold-start trade-off
* Kyverno policies to enforce responsible serverless usage
* When Knative is the right answer and when it is not
